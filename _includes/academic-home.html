Chao Wang (王超)

I am an Assistant Research Fellow at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Xidian University. I am also a member of the School of Artificial Intelligence and the Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding of Ministry of Education. My research focuses on evolutionary intelligence and black-box optimization, especially knowledge-driven meta-evolutionary optimization, open-ended neuroevolution, and evolutionary intelligence for scientific discovery.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Xidian University in June 2025, advised by Prof. Licheng Jiao and Prof. Jing Liu. My current postdoctoral co-advisor is Prof. Fang Liu.
